Enterprise Design System

The official design language, component library, and digital brand guidelines for MOH ZANNAH GROUP LIMITED. This living document ensures consistency, premium quality, and architectural scalability across all corporate digital properties.

Design Principles

Minimalist, executive, and focused on trust and authority.

Code Standards

Tailwind CSS utility-first approach with semantic HTML5.

Accessibility

WCAG compliant, high contrast ratios, and screen-reader friendly.

Brand Identity

Primary Logo

The primary corporate logo must always maintain its original aspect ratio. Do not alter colors or add unauthorized shadows directly to the asset.
